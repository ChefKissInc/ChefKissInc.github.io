It looks like Apple is slowly moving to DriverKit.

DriverKit is a replacement to IOKit and runs code in userspace instead of the kernel.

Currently this affects third party WiFi drivers on Sequoia.

We will not reveal exact details as to not help certain other third parties with their own software.
